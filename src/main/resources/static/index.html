How to Play

  • Goal: Be the last sponge on the platform!
  • Controls: Press SPACEBAR to launch your sponge.
  • Aim: A pointer spins around you. Time your launch carefully!
  • Power Up: The longer you wait, the bigger and more powerful you become.
  • Sudden Death: After 5 seconds, the arena will start to shrink!
  • Hit F5 To Play Again!
  • If you like Spong-Fu please star our repo here!
